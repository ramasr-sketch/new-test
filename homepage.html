Why Donate Blood?

  • One donation can save up to three lives.
  • Blood is essential for surgeries, accidents, and medical conditions.
  • Every donation helps those in need of critical care.
  • The donation process typically takes less than 10 minutes. The procedure is safe, and only a small amount (about a pint) of blood is taken. Donors are monitored to ensure their safety throughout the process.
